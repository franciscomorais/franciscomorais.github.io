Token acquisition (October)


This page has created to inform you how to proceed with the acquisition of Mint tokens. Before making your investment, please read the points below:

  1. Token acquisition fees will be divided by a percentage based on the investment of all participants in this acquisition.

  2. After acquiring the tokens, the user can request a withdrawal to a wallet that supports the token (ex: metamask). However, the cost of fees will be supported by the user.

  3. Please take a special attention that this is a high risk investment. The valorization or desvalorization of the token can happen in large percentage and the pool reaction will be very slow.
